Fun Facts

  • This game is a rematch of Super Bowl XLIX (2015), where the Patriots defeated the Seahawks 28–24 with Malcolm Butler's interception.
  • The Seahawks and Patriots have faced each other 21 times overall (regular season + playoffs), with Seattle slightly leading the series.
  • Super Bowl LX is the 10th rematch in Super Bowl history, a rare occurrence in NFL history.
  • The New England Patriots hold the NFL record for the most Super Bowl appearances by any franchise.
  • Levi’s Stadium hosted Super Bowl 50 in 2016 and now Super Bowl LX, making it one of the few stadiums to host multiple Super Bowls.
  • Bad Bunny is the first solo Spanish-speaking artist to headline the Super Bowl halftime show.
